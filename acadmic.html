Unit system

Total units for a course is 300 points. Units can distributed between the following-

  1. Core units- 100 credit points (compulsory units)
  2. First major units- 100 credit points
  3. Component units- Minor (4 total 50 points each), Electives(standalone)

Course planners contains information about the units and majors for specific course and can be used as guide when enrolling.
