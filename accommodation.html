Accommodation

Accommodation is one the most essential aspects to a university student, especially if you're an international student.

Can't make up your mind ? Consider the types of options we suggest below.

Types of choices

Swinburne Residences

Whether you're a college freshman, or have no idea at all deciding where you will be staying, consider choosing the on-campus options provided by Swinburne.

Living on-campus is the easiest and most convenient solution as it takes only around 2 minutes for you to walk from your place to the lecture halls. Besides, there are many facilities that are provided to you without additional costs. Futhermore, Swinburne Residences hosts social Groups frequently. This means you get to know and make friends from various backgrounds while having fun together.

You can choose to live on your own, or you can share with others with a maximum of 4 persons. While this may not be the most budget-friendly solution, at least you get to save yourself from headaches when deciding where to live.

Living off-campus

Want more freedom and control over what you prefer? If you have friends that are interested in living under the same roof, or perhaps you're just having a tight budget, renting off-campus is always an ideal option.

There are lots of resources where you can look for a place of your choice.

  • realestate.com.au
  • domain.com.au
  • rent.com.au
  • flatmates.com.au (If you don't mind living with a complete stranger)

and many more. You can even find listings on local newspapers, or join a Facebook group for more localised content.

While this might be a more budget-friendly solution, it is also the most complex considering there are lots of procedures in between after you have opted what you want. Fear not if you have no idea or little knowledge on what you should do. We've got you covered.

Homestay

If you're under 18 years old, or just wanted to experience the true Australian culture and lifestyle, then nothing suits you better than homestay. Besides getting to live with your host family, food and facilities are readily available as well. You will definitely feel less homesick with your new family.
