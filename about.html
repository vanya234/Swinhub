What is SwinHub?

SwinHub is an informative and collaborative platform that provides general information and advices to students regarding aspects including academic, finance, healthcare and many more. This can be useful for students who are looking for more information to solve their problems but struggle to search for a solution.

It is hoped that through this platform, students would get and provide help to others in need and ultimately forming a student community that provides a caring and positive environment for all students.
