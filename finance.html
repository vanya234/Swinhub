2. What if i don't pay my fees on time?

If the fees is not paid on time, you may either be cancelled from the degree or if you withdraw from the unit (after the due date), you will get a fail mark on your student record.

"It is your responsibility to ensure that your enrolment is correct prior to the due date".

Banking in Australia

What do you need to open a bank account in Australia?

  1. Personal identification (passport, birth certificate or similar)
  2. Proof of current address and contact details
  3. Money to deposit
  4. student visa and/or Confirmation of Enrolment [Depending On Bank]

    5. Note: if you are planning to work in Australia, you are required have a Tax File Number
