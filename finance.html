Banking

What do you need to open a bank account in Australia?

  • Personal identification (passport, birth certificate or similar)
  • Proof of current address and contact details
  • Money to deposit
  • Student visa and/or Confirmation of Enrolment (Depending On Bank)

Note: If you are planning to work in Australia, you are required have a Tax File Number.
